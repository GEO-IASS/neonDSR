Pathfinder meteorological data

A portible weather station is used to collect temperature, pressure, 
relative humidity, and wind speed.

File name convention:

YYMMDD_weather.txt

	YY    - two-digit year of measurement
	MM    - two-digit month of measurement
	DD    - two-digit day of measurement

File columns:

1. YYYY-MM-DD, date of measurement
2. HH:MM:SS, time of measurement
3. Digital time in seconds
4. Magnetic wind direction
5. True wind direction
6. Wind speed (mph)
7. Crosswind (mph)
8. Headwind/Tailwind (mph)
9. Temperature (C)
10. Wind chill (C)
12. Relative humidity (%)
11. Heat index (C)
12. Dew point (C)
13. Wet bulb (C)
14. Barometric pressure (mb)
15. Altitude (m)
16. Density altitude (m)
